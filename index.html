It's a Deal: An Analysis of All Shark Tank Pitches



Overview

  • As of early February 2024, the sharks heard 1,325 pitches and invested in 60% of them.
  • Across all deals, 84% have been standard, involving only capital for equity. The rest included a royalty, loan, or some combination.
  • The average standard deal was $282,550 for 26%, which computed a value of $1.08 million on the business.
  • Almost a quarter (22%) of pitches were food and beverage, and it was the sector that got the most deals too.
  • Mark Cuban made 243 deals, which is more than any other shark. He was involved in 30% of all deals, as of Feb. 2, 2024.
